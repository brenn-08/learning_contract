Learning Contract

EXPECTATIONS

  • I expect to gain a strong foundation in the principles and concepts of web information systems, including both the frontend and backend aspects.
  • I expect to develop my problem-solving skills, which may help address challenges that may arise.
  • I expect to improve my time management skills and prioritize the tasks given in this course effectively.
  • I expect to develop my communication skills, particularly by working with team members and on projects.
  • I expect to achieve at least a passing grade and afterward, continue to apply these skills.

CONTRIBUTIONS

  • I will contribute to the class by attending classes regularly.
  • I will contribute to the class by doing my assignments and tasks.
  • I will collaborate with my classmates and develop the skills we want to enhance.
  • I will listen to the professor attentively during discussions.
  • I will respect the rules and guidelines to eliminate disruptions during classes.

MOTIVATIONS

  • I want to improve my coding skills and prepare for real-world web information system projects.
  • One of my motivations to attain our goals in this course is my family, who continuously supports and guides us.
  • I want to achieve a passing grade in this course.
  • I want to continuously improve in our field and eventually graduate.
  • I want to gain more experience while taking advantage of the opportunity to collaborate with our classmates.

HINDRANCES

  • Possible hindrance is having an overwhelming workload that may burn us out and reduces our focus in studying.
  • Another hindrance that may affect our learning is our mental health, as we tend to get anxious during presentations or reporting.
  • An external hindrance that may hinder our learning is our unreliable internet connection or power outage in our area, especially during online classes.
  • Confusion with coding concepts can be a hindrance.
  • Time management challenges with multiple course requirements can affect our work.
